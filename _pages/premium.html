Frequently asked questions

What can I do with premium?
  • Create as many groups and expenses as you need
  • Scan receipts directly into groups to settle up later
  • Allow friends to create and edit expenses in your groups
  • Do everything you can without premium
What can I do without premium?
  • Scan receipts and send an image of balances
  • Join as many groups as you need
  • Create expenses in groups that have a premium group member
  • Scan receipts directly into groups that have a premium group member
  • Settle up in any group (including ones with no premium member)
Do I need premium to create a group?

Yes, only people with premium can create groups.

Do I need premium to join a group?

Nope! You can join as many groups as you need, for free.

As long as at least one other person in the group has premium, everyone in the group can create and edit expenses in that group.

Are there limits on how many groups or expenses I can have?

For the most part, no! You can create as many groups and expenses as you need.

We may take action in rare cases where we believe someone is abusing the service.

What happens when there are no premium members in a group?

The group becomes locked. Nothing is deleted, but no expenses can be created or edited (except settling up) until there’s a premium member in the group again.

Can I settle up in a locked group?

Yes! You can settle up, but cannot create or edit other expenses until there’s a premium member in the group again.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

You can cancel your premium subscription in the App Store or Play Store (whichever one you bought it from).

There’s also a link to manage your subscription on the settings page in the Heypenny app, to make things easy.
